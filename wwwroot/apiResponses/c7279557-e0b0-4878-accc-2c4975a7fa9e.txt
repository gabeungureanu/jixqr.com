**Scene 3 - "Variables of Values"**

Jordan's office is quiet except for the rhythmic tapping of her keyboard. Alex stands by her desk, a furrow creasing his brow as he holds a printout of code. The fluorescent lights hum softly overhead, casting a clinical glow over the room. 

Jordan pauses, noticing Alex's hesitation. "What's on your mind, Alex?" she asks without looking up from her screen.

Alex shifts his weight from one foot to the other. "It's this section here," he points at a nested loop on the page. "I can't seem to optimize it. And I keep wondering... am I really cut out for this?"

Jordan stops typing and swivels her chair to face him. She takes the paper, her eyes scanning the lines of code. "Let's walk through it together," she says, her tone patient.

They huddle around the whiteboard as Jordan picks up a marker. "Think of each variable as a piece of a larger puzzle," she begins, her hand drawing an array on the board. "Each has its place and purpose, just like us. We're here to contribute our unique strengths to the project."

"But what if my 'variable' is flawed?" Alex's voice is barely above a whisper, his self-doubt palpable.

Jordan caps the marker and turns to him, her gaze steady. "In code, as in life, we refactor. We learn and improve. You're not flawed, Alex—you're evolving. And that's what makes a great programmer."

Alex's eyes hold onto hers, a flicker of understanding dawning in them. "And our values?" he probes further, "How do we ensure they don't get lost in the process?"

Jordan leans against the whiteboard, her arms folded. "That's the challenge, isn't it?" she muses. "But it's like writing clean code. You keep the end goal in mind, stay true to the logic—your values—and everything else aligns."

A smile tugs at the corner of Alex's mouth. "So, I refactor my code and my doubts?"

"Exactly," Jordan nods, a smile of her own spreading across her face. "Now, let's tackle this bug together."

They dive back into the code, their dialogue a symphony of problem-solving and mutual respect. The scene closes on their concentrated faces, a testament to the growth unfolding within the walls of the tech company.
